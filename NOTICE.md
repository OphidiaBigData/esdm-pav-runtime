ESDM-PAV Runtime General
----------------------

The ESDM-PAV runtime support the execution of data post-processing, analytics and visualisation (PAV) experiments in the field of climate and weather applications. Its core modules are based on the [Ophidia Server](https://github.com/OphidiaBigData/ophidia-server)
